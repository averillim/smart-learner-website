About us

Teaching students has become harder as a result of the COVID-19 outbreak, where the majority of individuals are being asked to remain at home in a state of quarantine. As we all know, education is an important part of every child's life because it moulds them into contributing members of society as adults. Therefore, Smart Learner is a type of application that presents a new way for them to learn online. Smart Learner used an artificial intelligence tutoring system that provides a human-like lesson.

  • Users are able to customise the AI to their preferences
  • The timing would be more flexible

Why Choose Us

The AI system we provide

The AI tutoring system provides the students with a human-like tutor without the hassle of finding one

It examines the student's strengths and weaknesses in the subject they are studying. If the student wishes to use a tutor, the tutor would already be aware of the student's areas of expertise and weaknesses.

The mode of learning we provide

We also provide a variety of modes for students to learn

Whenever the students get bored with their current mode of learning, they can switch it up by playing games related to the topic. It is very useful for younger children, as they have a shorter attention span.
